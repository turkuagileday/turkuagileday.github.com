Registration

The information on this page is outdated and/or unofficial. Registrations will not be processed until we officially open the registration period.

Please fill in the registration form below and register to the event. The amount of participants is limited for both workshops and the main event.

We have hotel allotments for our guests for the period of the conference. The hotel information & discount codes are given in an email sent to You once the registration is confirmed.

Once posting the registration form, You will be sent a confirmation email containing a link which validates the given email address and confirms Your registration. As a return, You will get another email with registration information and the invoice.
