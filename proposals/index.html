Call for proposals

Turku Agile Day is back! We’re bringing you again the coziest software conference in Finland! The event will take place on May 12th-13th (Monday-Tuesday) in ICT-Talo in Turku and it will be one day of workshops (Monday) and one day of talks (Tuesday) and, of course, time to network on both days.

As usual, we don’t have any single theme for the conference. Instead, we would like to have a wide range of workshops and talks spanning from soft skills to hardcore technical stuff. So don’t be afraid to submit your talk! Here are some general themes that we’re looking for this year:

  • Testing / Development / Devops etc
  • Management / Company culture etc
  • Soft skills / Mindset etc
  • Agile in embedded systems
  • Academic research

Submit your proposal!

Send your proposal to: info@turkuagileday.fi
