Testing with the Stars

What it is

"Testing with the Stars" (loosely modeled on the popular TV show "Dancing with the Stars") is a testing competition quite like Programming with the Stars held in Agile2009. The purpose of the competition is to promote testing skills, techniques and how a tester can help agile team to build software that matters. For someone. And to have fun!

When & where

Tuesday May 15th 2012 from 9:30 to 16:30
(Semi-)Final on Wednesday May 16th 2012
