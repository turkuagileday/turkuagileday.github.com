Turku Agile Day

Turku Agile Day 2010

Turku Agile Day 2010 was held 17th to 18th March in 2010. It was the first time the Turku Agile Day was a two day conference, first day featuring the workshops and second day featuring the talks.

Workshops

  • Exploratory Testing by Maaret Pyhäjärvi
  • Lean Manufacturing Game by Taru Salmimaa and Lasse Ziegler
  • Test Driven Development by Lasse Koskela
  • Improving and Maintaining Code Quality by Petri Louhelainen
  • Acceptance TDD by Example by Pekka Klärck, Juha Rantanen, Janne Härkönen
  • Business Value Game by Tobias Anderberg, Ola Ellnestam
  • Coding Dojo by Markus Hjort
  • Continuous Integration by Thomas Sundberg

Talks

  • keynote: Pinocchio: On Becoming a Lean Leader by Portia Tung and Pascal Van Cauwenberghe
  • How to Sell Agile to my Managers by Marko Taipale
  • Flow where you can, pull where you must - a practitioners guide to Kanban by Joakim Sundén
  • Transformational Agile Testing Practices: Lessons with Legacy by Maaret Pyhäjärvi
  • Phronetic Leadership - Or how to lead knowledge companies by Ola Ellnestam
  • Timeboxed Thinking by Portia Tung and Staffan Nöteberg
  • Agile is easy, it’s making it work with your business that is hard by Vasco Duarte
  • Case: Agile Usability - Experiences in combining UI design and agile development by Sami Ekblad
  • Train Your Customer by Petri Heiramo
  • keynote Agile: Get Ready by Elisabeth Hendrickson
